<p><p>The Bone Preparation Kit market comprises two main types: needle and needleless systems. Needle systems involve traditional methods requiring a needle for aspiration and injection of materials into the bone, commonly used in various orthopedic and dental procedures. In contrast, needleless technologies utilize advanced mechanisms to deliver bone grafts or other materials without the use of needles, enhancing safety and reducing patient discomfort. Both types cater to specific procedural needs, with needleless options gaining popularity for their ease of use and reduced risk of infection.</p></p>
