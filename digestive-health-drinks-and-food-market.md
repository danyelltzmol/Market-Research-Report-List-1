<p><p>The digestive health drinks and food market encompasses three main types: prebiotics, probiotics, and food enzymes. Probiotics are live microorganisms that, when consumed in adequate amounts, support gut flora balance and overall well-being. Food enzymes assist in breaking down food components, improving nutrient absorption and digestion.
