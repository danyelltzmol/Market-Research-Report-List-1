<p><p>The Aircraft and Aerospace Composite Panels market consists of various types, primarily including sandwich panels and high-temperature panels. Sandwich panels are structured with a lightweight core material, typically between two composite facings, providing excellent strength-to-weight ratios and thermal insulation, making them ideal for aircraft interiors and fuselage applications. High-temperature panels are designed to withstand extreme temperatures and are used in critical aerospace components, ensuring durability and safety in demanding environments, such as engine components and thermal protection systems.</p></p>
