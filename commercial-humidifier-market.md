<p><p>The commercial humidifier market features various types, including vapor-type and water spray humidifiers. Vapor-type humidifiers generate steam through boiling water, releasing warm, purified humidity into the air, making them suitable for larger spaces. In contrast, water spray humidifiers disperse fine water droplets, quickly increasing humidity levels without raising temperatures, ideal for sensitive environments. Both types play critical roles in maintaining optimal humidity, enhancing comfort, and preserving equipment and products across various commercial applications.</p></p>
