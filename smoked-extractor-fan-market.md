<p><p>The smoked extractor fan market is primarily divided into three types: centrifugal, axial, and others. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to generate high pressure and are ideal for moving air through long ducts. Axial fans, on the other hand, use blades to push air parallel to the fan axis, making them suitable for ventilation with lower resistance. The "others" category encompasses specialized fans, such as mixed flow or inline fans, which cater to unique applications and specific airflow requirements.</p></p>
