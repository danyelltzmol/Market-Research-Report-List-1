<p><ul><li>Vertical Axis</li><li>Horizontal Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Trailed Aerator market is primarily categorized into two types: Vertical Axis and Horizontal Axis aerators. Vertical Axis aerators utilize a vertical rotation mechanism to stir and aerate soil, enhancing oxygen availability and promoting healthy root development. In contrast, Horizontal Axis aerators feature a horizontal rotation that allows for broader coverage and efficient soil mixing. Both types are designed to improve soil structure and fertility but cater to different agricultural needs and terrains, influencing their market demand and application.</p></p>
